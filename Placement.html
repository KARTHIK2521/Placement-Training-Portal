Placement Process

  • Campus placement training (HR, Technical, GD, Written Test and C, C++ program) is given in pre-final years for all eligible candidates through

    • Consultants for inputs

    • Staff for practice

    • Placement officer for fine tuning

  • Conducts soft skills training program (100 hours) for all pre final year students through our staff trained on Infosys Campus Connect concept.

  • Conducts Cambridge ESOL-BEC Training of 70 Hrs & Certification.

  • Department placement coordinators assist campus interviews and training programs.

  • Arranges training program on soft skills and technical subjects for staff and students through corporates.

  • Conducts on line test for the improvement of Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning, and Verbal Ability.
